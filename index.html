** Markdown Markdown **

Stonefruit is built on markdown — a lightweight way of typing that turns plain text into richer documents. Why this matters to you: Your notes are just files. Plain text files. If you start using Stonefruit and later decide you don't like it, there's the door. Take all your notes with you. No lock-in. It also turns out markdown is a very model-friendly format, which matters once we get to the overnight part.
